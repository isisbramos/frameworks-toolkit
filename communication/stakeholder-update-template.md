# Stakeholder Update Template

## When to use
Weekly or bi-weekly async updates. Keeps stakeholders informed without scheduling meetings.

## [Project Name] Update - [Date]

### Status: On Track / At Risk / Blocked

### Summary
One paragraph on where things stand.

### Progress this period
What got done since the last update.

### Planned next
What the team is focusing on next.

### Blockers or risks
Anything that needs attention or escalation.

### Decisions needed
List any decisions stakeholders need to make.

## Tips
Keep it scannable. Be honest about blockers early. Send at a consistent cadence.
