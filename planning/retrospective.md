# Retrospective Template

## When to use
End of sprint, cycle, project, or quarter. A structured way to reflect and improve.

## Format

### What went well?
Celebrate wins, big and small.

### What didn't go well?
Be honest, not personal. Focus on process, not people.

### What did we learn?
Insights that should change how we work.

### What will we do differently?
Concrete actions with owners. Max 3.

| Action | Owner | Due |
|--------|-------|-----|
| | | |

## Tips

- Timebox to 45-60 minutes
- - Psychological safety is non-negotiable
  - - Follow up on action items in the next retro
    - - Rotate facilitators to keep it fresh
